Importance

My digital portfolio is important to me because it lets me see all my projects throughout my Computer Science classes at AHS.

Also, I can present my projects to potential employers in a digital format. Companies will want to know more about me than the college I went to or the grades I achiveed in my classes. Using this Digital Portfolio, I can present my past work to my employers, which will help companies understand why I can be a valuable asset to any company in need of software expertise.

Additionally, I will use my Digital Portfolio to organize my work in computer science. my Digital Portfolio will help me prioritize the projects I'm more interested in and motivate me to finish those projects to improve my Digital Portfolio

Throughout my computer scince journey, I'll also be able to look back on my Digital Portfolio at the end of this year or years from now. This willl let me see my progress as a programmer, similar to how an artist can measure improvement through the progression of their physical portfolio.

My Digital Portfolio can even serve as documentation. For each of my projects in my Digital Portfolio, I'll need to explain why I worked on them and what purpose they serve. That way, if I ever come back to my old projects, I'll understand them much more quickly without having to look at the code behind them.

Because my Digital Portfolio is itself a project, making it will improve my ability to work independently. This includes using Google to search effectively and writing effective code to operate my website.

Finally, I can demonstrate my abilities in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through the design and interactivity of my website. This gives me another way to show employers my skills, as website development is still a valuable skill. Especially as I improve in these languages, the design of my website will speak for itself.
