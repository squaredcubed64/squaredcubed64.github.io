Importance

My digital portfolio is important to me because it lets me see all my projects throughout my Computer Science classes at AHS. With my digital portfolio, I can study my growth over this year and across years.

Also, I can present my projects to potential employers in a digital format. Companies will want to know more about me than the college I went to or the grades I achiveed in my classes. Using this Digital Portfolio, I can present my past work to my employers, which will help companies understand why I can be a valuable asset to any company in need of software expertise.

Finally, I can demonstrate my abilities in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through the design and interactivity of my website. This gives me another way to show employers my skills, as website development is still a valuable skill. Especially as I improve in these languages, the design of my website will speak for itself.
